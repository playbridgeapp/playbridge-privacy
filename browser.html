Privacy Policy for PlayBridge Browser Last updated: March 25, 2026 Overview PlayBridge Browser is a web browser app for Android TV that allows you to browse the web from your TV, controlled from your phone. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with any server, service, or third party. Data stored on your device PlayBridge Browser stores the following data locally on your device only: - App settings — Browser preferences and configuration, stored using Android's DataStore API on the device itself. Network usage PlayBridge Browser loads web pages and content from URLs you navigate to. All browsing activity stays between your device and the websites you visit — no data is sent to the developer. The app receives navigation commands from the PlayBridge TV app running on the same device over a local broadcast — no network communication leaves your device for this purpose. Permissions - Internet / Network access — Required to load web pages. - Foreground service — Keeps the browser available to receive navigation commands while running. - Notifications — Shows a status notification while the browser service is running. Third-party services PlayBridge Browser uses GeckoView (Mozilla's browser engine) to render web pages. GeckoView's own privacy policy applies to its operation. PlayBridge Browser does not use any analytics, advertising, or crash reporting SDKs. Children's privacy PlayBridge Browser does not knowingly collect any information from children.
